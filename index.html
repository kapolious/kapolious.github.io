ABOUT ME

As a computer science student, I find knitting to be a therapeutic break from my screen-based work. The manual work of knitting allows me to slow down and focus on the tangible task at hand.


Despite their apparent differences, knitting and computer science share many similarities. Both require attention to detail, patience, and problem-solving skills.
